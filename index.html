Problem

It's hard to keep track of the food in your fridge. Moreover, each food has an expiration date. It is difficult to find recipes that contain only your ingredients. The problem is that there is no application to help people remember all these things and help them in the kitchen according to their needs.

Solution

A single application for all these problems

Our idea is a customized food assistant that propose you recipes based on the food you already have. It takes into account of the expire dates and minimize food waste

There's a lot more features ready to be discovered!

Food Tracking

Ingredients Planning

CUSTOMER SEGMENTS

Our app will serve all the people who want to eat healthy and prepare meal themself without wasting the food already bought.

Target customers are people who like to cook but they don't have time to search new recipes and, also, they can't keep track all of food from their fridge.

KEY Advantages
An all-in-one solution that implements the most important features of every important competitor: recipes generation, fridge and products synchronization, food waste reduction
Simple and intuitive UI to easily navigate between the main features of the app
Go to the next level with comprehensive voice assistant capable of recognizing thousands of products
Meet Chefy, your customized avatar that tracks the progress of your eating habits and recipes history to offer you better suggestions each time you enter the app
Use Drinker: matches the desired recipe with a compatible drink (e.g. match a dish with a specific type of wine)
