IDEA

Eatics is a web application capable of offering personalized recipes based on the products already in the fridge, taking into account the expire dates of each product in order to reduce food waste.

We came up with some interesting possible features of our application, which would transform our solution into an all-in-one smart kitchen assistant, capable of guiding us through the whole process, from the moment we decide what we need to buy to the moment when we cook our dishes in an efficient and easy way.
