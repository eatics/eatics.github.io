IDEA

We thought that a web application capable of offering you recipes based on the products you already have in the fridge, taking into account the expire dates of each product in order to reduce food waste would be a great start in solving the main problems that we had found before.

Also, we came up with some interesting possible features of our application, which would transform our solution into an all-in-one smart kitchen assistant, capable of guiding us through the whole process, from the moment we decide what we need to buy to the moment when we cook our dishes in an efficient and easy way.

MAIN ADVANTAGES
  • An all-in-one solution that implements the most important features of every important competitor: recipes generation, fridge and products synchronization, food waste reduction
  • Simple and intuitive UI to easily navigate between the main features of the app
  • Go to the next level with comprehensive voice assistant capable of recognizing thousands of products
  • Meet Chefy, your customized avatar that tracks the progress of your eating habits and recipes history to offer you better suggestions each time you enter the app
  • Use Drinker: matches the desired recipe with a compatible drink (e.g. match a dish with a specific type of wine)
